Northwind Atlas G2 — Installation Guide

This guide covers the electrical and mounting requirements for the Northwind Atlas G2 thermostat. Installation should be performed by a qualified technician after disconnecting power at the breaker.

Power requirements

The Northwind Atlas G2 requires a 24-volt common wire (C-wire) for continuous power. It will not operate on battery power alone. If no C-wire is present, install the included Northwind power-bridge adapter at the HVAC control board.

Mounting

The device mounts to a standard single-gang electrical box. Use the supplied trim plate to cover legacy thermostat cut-outs. The base must be level for the internal temperature sensor to read accurately.
