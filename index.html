Northwind Atlas G2 — Product Handbook

The Northwind Atlas G2 is a connected smart thermostat designed for small commercial spaces. This handbook is the canonical reference for warranty, installation, and operating specifications. It is maintained by the Northwind Atlas product team and updated each release cycle.

Warranty

The Northwind Atlas G2 thermostat carries a manufacturer warranty of 36 months from the date of purchase. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. It does not cover damage from improper installation, power surges, or unauthorized firmware.

Overview

The G2 supports seven-day scheduling, geofencing, and remote management through the Northwind Atlas dashboard. It is compatible with single-stage and two-stage heating and cooling systems. The device reports temperature, humidity, and runtime telemetry at five-minute intervals.
